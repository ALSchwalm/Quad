Quad

How To Play

The objective of Quad is to place 'Quads' on a game grid. Each quad is composed of 4 colored blocks. Create groups of blocks of the same color to clear blocks and earn points!

Key Action
Left/Right Rotate the waiting quad clockwise/counter-clockwise
WASDShift the contents of the grid
SpaceDrop the waiting quad
